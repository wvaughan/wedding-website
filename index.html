Our Story

The Bride

Zara’s first love was dinosaurs, but has since healed and moved on. In her free time, you can find her doing arts and crafts, going for a hike, reading a book, petting animals, riding a bike, lamenting how she doesn’t bike more often, or Googling pictures of birds with arms. She grew up in Congers, NY with two wonderful parents, Cory and Carol, and a younger brother, Jacob, who miraculously still talks to her.

She is a proud graduate of Congers Elementary School, Clarkstown High School North, and a survivor of Felix Festa Middle School. After high school, she attended Tufts University where she studied English and Mass Communications, and played Quidditch for the Tufts Tufflepuffs. She pursues her love of writing as a content strategist in San Francisco.

The Groom

Will’s greatest joys in life include high-speed Internet, low-fidelity design mocks, Bill Belichick’s salty demeanor, whoopie pies, traveling, and definitely not vertical videos. He grew up in Lynnfield, MA with the legendary Bill and Sharon, and a howlin’ good succession of beagles, Mandy, Misty, and Brandy. He can trace his academic success to Huckleberry Elementary School (not Summer Street), where the precocious youngster dove right into 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea, while Zara was still stuck in the Magic Tree House series.

After graduating valedictorian of Lynnfield High School, he also went on to Tufts University, where he studied the totally real field of Engineering Psychology, and studied abroad at the Universidad de Alcalá. He now works as a user experience designer of robots, which he assures everyone will not be taking over the world (yet).

Their Powers Combined

It was love at second sight. Will and Zara met at Tufts, where they both carried on learning stuff and not dating. Fate or geographic probability then threw them together again at athenahealth, where Zara recognized him, developed a crush, and kept giving him her number until, tech be praised, he finally put it in his phone. They shared a few wonderful years with family and friends on the east coast and then up and left for California, where they're currently enjoying the lack of snow and ice.
